Early Detection Saves Lives

If you notice any new, changing, or unusual skin growths, seek immediate medical attention. Skin cancer is highly treatable when detected early.

Skin Cancer Overview

Skin cancer is the most common form of cancer worldwide, but it's also one of the most preventable and treatable when detected early. At Yuva Skin Clinic, we provide comprehensive skin cancer screening, diagnosis, and treatment services with compassion and expertise.

Our board-certified dermatologists use advanced diagnostic tools and follow evidence-based treatment protocols to ensure the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Types of Skin Cancer

Most Common

Basal Cell Carcinoma (BCC)

Slow-growing cancer that rarely spreads but can cause significant local damage if untreated.

80% of skin cancers
Rarely metastasizes
High cure rate
Second Most Common

Squamous Cell Carcinoma (SCC)

Can grow more quickly than BCC and has a higher risk of spreading to other parts of the body.

20% of skin cancers
Can metastasize
Early treatment crucial
Most Dangerous

Melanoma

The most serious type of skin cancer that can spread quickly to other organs if not treated early.

1% of skin cancers
High metastasis risk
Early detection vital

Seek Shade

Avoid direct sun between 10 AM and 4 PM when UV rays are strongest
