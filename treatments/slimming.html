Body Slimming Overview

Whether you're looking to reduce love handles, contour your abdomen, slim your thighs, or sculpt your arms, we offer personalized treatment plans to help you achieve your body goals safely and effectively.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Most treatments are comfortable with minimal discomfort. Cryolipolysis feels like intense cold followed by numbness. Radiofrequency feels like a warm massage. We ensure maximum comfort throughout the procedure with advanced technology and techniques.

    Most areas require 1-2 sessions of cryolipolysis or 6-10 sessions of radiofrequency/ultrasound. Multiple small areas can be treated in one session. During your consultation, we'll create a personalized treatment plan based on your goals and body type.

    Initial changes appear in 3-4 weeks. Optimal results are visible at 2-3 months as your body naturally eliminates the treated fat cells. Radiofrequency shows immediate skin tightening with cumulative results over sessions.

    Most treatments have zero downtime. You can return to normal activities immediately. Some patients experience temporary redness, swelling, or numbness that resolves within hours to days. We provide detailed aftercare instructions.

    Yes, fat cells eliminated through treatments are permanently removed. However, remaining fat cells can still expand with weight gain. Maintaining a stable weight through healthy lifestyle ensures long-lasting results. Annual maintenance sessions can help maintain optimal results.

    Yes, we can treat multiple areas in one session or consecutive sessions. Popular combinations include abdomen + love handles, thighs + buttocks, or arms + back. We offer package deals for multiple area treatments.

    Cost varies by treatment type and areas treated. Small areas start at ₹5,000 per session, medium areas from ₹8,000, large areas from ₹12,000. We offer package discounts and financing options. Consultation includes detailed cost estimate.

    Pregnant or breastfeeding women, individuals with certain medical conditions (liver disease, blood disorders), those with loose skin or severe obesity. We conduct thorough medical screening to ensure safety and optimal results.

    Important Information

    Not a weight loss solution: Body slimming treatments target specific areas of stubborn fat, not overall weight loss. Maintain healthy diet and exercise for best results.

    Realistic expectations: Results vary based on individual factors. Multiple sessions may be needed for desired results. Follow post-treatment instructions carefully.
