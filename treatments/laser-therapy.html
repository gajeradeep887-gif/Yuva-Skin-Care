Laser Therapy Overview

Laser therapy uses concentrated beams of light to target specific skin concerns with precision and effectiveness. Our advanced laser systems deliver controlled energy to stimulate collagen production, remove unwanted pigmentation, and rejuvenate your skin from within.

We utilize FDA-approved, state-of-the-art laser technology that's safe for all skin types. Our certified laser specialists customize treatments based on your unique skin characteristics and treatment goals.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Most patients describe the sensation as a rubber band snap or mild pinprick. We use advanced cooling technology and topical numbing cream to maximize comfort. Discomfort is temporary and subsides immediately after treatment.

    Most conditions require 3-6 sessions spaced 4-6 weeks apart. Hair removal typically needs 6-8 sessions. Deep wrinkles or scars may require additional treatments. Your personalized treatment plan will be determined during consultation.

    Non-ablative lasers have no downtime. Fractional CO₂ requires 3-7 days of social downtime. Most patients can return to work within 2-3 days. We provide detailed aftercare instructions to minimize recovery time.

    Yes, our advanced lasers are safe for all skin types (Fitzpatrick I-VI). We customize wavelength, pulse duration, and energy settings based on your skin type and tone. A patch test is performed before full treatment.

    Initial improvement is visible within 1-2 weeks. Optimal results appear after 2-3 months as collagen production increases. Hair removal shows progressive reduction with each session, with final results after the full course.

    Hair removal results are permanent for treated follicles. Skin rejuvenation results last 2-5 years, but aging continues naturally. Maintenance treatments can prolong results. We provide long-term care plans.

    Temporary redness, swelling, and mild discomfort are common. Rare side effects include temporary hyperpigmentation or hypopigmentation. Serious complications are extremely rare with proper technique and equipment.

    Cost varies by treatment area and number of sessions. Small areas start at ₹2,000 per session, full face from ₹8,000. Package discounts are available. During consultation, we'll provide a detailed cost estimate.

    Safety & Contraindications

    Not suitable if: Pregnant or breastfeeding, have active skin infections, recent sun exposure, history of keloid scarring, or certain medical conditions. Avoid retinoids, blood thinners, and photosensitizing medications before treatment. Always disclose complete medical history.
