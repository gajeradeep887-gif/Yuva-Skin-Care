Hair Transplant Overview

Hair transplant is a permanent surgical solution for hair loss that involves transferring hair follicles from the donor area (back and sides of the head) to the bald or thinning areas. It's the most effective solution for pattern baldness, offering natural-looking, permanent results.

At Yuva Skin Clinic, we use advanced techniques performed by board-certified hair transplant surgeons to ensure maximum graft survival, natural hairline design, and optimal density for a completely natural appearance.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    The procedure is performed under local anesthesia, so you won't feel pain during the surgery. Some discomfort may be experienced during anesthesia injection and post-procedure. We provide pain medication to manage any discomfort during recovery.

    Depending on the number of grafts, the procedure takes 4-8 hours. We perform it in a single session for most cases. You'll be awake during the procedure and can watch movies, listen to music, or rest comfortably.

    Most patients return to work in 3-5 days. If your job involves physical labor, you may need 7-10 days. The tiny scabs that form are usually not noticeable after 5-7 days, and you can style your hair to cover them.

    Yes, transplanted hair is permanent as it's taken from the permanent donor area (back and sides of head) which is genetically resistant to balding. However, existing natural hair may continue to thin, so medication may be recommended to preserve it.

    With modern techniques and experienced surgeons, results are completely natural. We design hairline based on your facial structure, age, and donor availability. Grafts are placed at correct angles and directions to match your natural hair growth pattern.

    Yes, women are excellent candidates for hair transplant if they have stable donor areas. Female pattern hair loss typically causes diffuse thinning, but we can transplant hair to increase density in thinning areas with excellent results.

    Cost is calculated per graft and varies by technique (₹40-80 per graft). A typical procedure of 2500-3500 grafts costs ₹1-3 lakhs. We provide detailed cost breakdown during consultation and offer financing options.

    FUE leaves tiny dot scars that are not visible even with short hair. FUT leaves a linear scar that can be hidden under hair. With proper technique and sufficient donor hair, scars are minimal and well-concealed.

    Important Information

    Not a cosmetic procedure: Hair transplant is a surgical procedure requiring proper evaluation. Good donor area is essential for success.

    Realistic expectations: Results depend on donor hair quality, surgeon skill, and aftercare. Multiple sessions may be needed for extensive baldness.
