Chemical Peels Overview

Chemical peels are professional skin treatments that use medical-grade acids to exfoliate and rejuvenate the skin. By removing damaged outer layers, they stimulate collagen production and reveal smoother, brighter, and more youthful skin underneath.

At Yuva Skin Clinic, we offer a range of chemical peels tailored to your specific skin concerns, from superficial peels for maintenance to medium-depth peels for more significant improvements.

The Peeling Process

Day 1

Treatment Day

Chemical solution applied, slight tingling sensation. Skin may appear red immediately after.

Day 2-3

Tightening Phase

Skin feels tight and may start to darken. Begin gentle cleansing and moisturizing.

Day 3-5

Peeling Begins

Visible peeling starts around mouth and nose. Never pull or peel skin manually.

Day 5-7

Peaking Peeling

Maximum peeling occurs. Skin underneath is pink and sensitive. Strict sun protection needed.

Day 7-10

Healing Complete

Peeling stops, revealing smoother, brighter skin. Can resume normal skincare routine.

Frequently Asked Questions

Most patients need 3-6 sessions spaced 2-4 weeks apart for optimal results. Light peels may require 4-6 sessions, while medium peels often show significant improvement in 2-3 sessions. Maintenance peels are recommended every 3-6 months.

During the peel, you'll feel a tingling or warm sensation that lasts 2-5 minutes. Light peels are generally comfortable, while medium peels may cause more intense sensations. We use fans and cooling techniques to maximize comfort during the procedure.

Light peels have no downtime - you can return to normal activities immediately. Medium peels require 3-7 days of social downtime as peeling occurs. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and can schedule treatments before weekends if needed.

Initial brightness is visible immediately after light peels. For medium peels, results become apparent after peeling completes (5-7 days). Optimal results are seen after 2-3 weeks as collagen continues to rebuild. Multiple sessions provide cumulative benefits.

After light peels, you can wear makeup the same day. After medium peels, wait until peeling completes (5-7 days). We recommend mineral-based makeup for the first week and provide specific product recommendations during your consultation.

Not suitable for pregnant or breastfeeding women, those with active cold sores, skin infections, or who have used Accutane in the last 6 months. Certain medical conditions and medications may also contraindicate treatment. We conduct a thorough assessment before proceeding.

Cost varies by peel type and number of sessions. Light peels start at ₹1,500 per session, medium peels from ₹3,500. We offer package discounts for multiple sessions. During consultation, we'll provide a detailed cost estimate based on your specific needs.

Yes, but sun protection is crucial. We recommend peels during cooler months or if you can strictly avoid sun exposure. We provide medical-grade sunscreens and detailed sun protection protocols for summer treatments.

Important Pre & Post Care Instructions

Before Treatment: Stop retinoids 5-7 days prior. Avoid sun exposure, waxing, and chemical depilatories for 2 weeks. Disclose all medications and supplements.

After Treatment: Use gentle cleanser and moisturizer. Apply SPF 50+ daily. Avoid direct sun, heat, sweating, and picking at skin. Follow specific aftercare instructions based on your peel type.
